Layout briefMost important
Hero
Large circular cashback badge — "₹1,000 Cashback" — occupying ~35% of the upper-centre frame, gold-outlined on a deep navy/charcoal background. The ₹1,000 numeral must be rendered at minimum 72px bold so it's legible at mobile thumb size without zoom. Below the badge, a flagship smartphone (generic, no specific brand) angled 3/4 view occupies 40% of the lower-centre frame — Layer 1 supporting context, signalling category relevance immediately.
Card
Bottom-left corner, approximately 18–22% of frame width. Card angled at 10–15 degrees for dynamism. Must be fully legible — do not crop card face. Default bottom-left placement (data: 2.97% vs 2.75% for bottom-right).
Background
Deep navy to charcoal gradient (#0D1B2A to #1C2B3A), with a very subtle radial bokeh glow behind the cashback badge in warm gold/amber (#C9A84C at 30% opacity). Not a full festive bokeh — restrained, premium. No white or light background — smartphone products pop best against dark.
Chips
₹1,000 Cashback on First Purchase EMI Starting ₹999/month ₹0 Down Payment ₹0 Annual Fee Up to ₹3,00,000 Loan Limit
Density
High — minimum 5 visible text elements (cashback badge, EMI chip, ₹0 Down chip, ₹0 Annual Fee chip, ₹3L limit chip). Chips pill-shaped, white text on semi-transparent dark background with a 1px white border, arranged vertically in the right 30% column. Headline copy placed above the phone image in 24–28px bold white.
Brand logo
Bajaj Finserv logo, top-right corner, white version, approximately 80px wide. No partner logo — this is a card-direct offer creative, not co-branded.
